About Me

Yo, I’m Kanye — not that Kanye, but I too believe I’m a God… right up until I open my dev console. I build websites with the confidence of “Can’t Tell Me Nothing” and the stability of Kanye’s Twitter feed. If your screen’s broken, it’s not my code — “Welcome to the Jungle,” baby.

I don’t test — I just feel the bugs. “I’m living in that 21st century, doing something mean to it”… like breaking layouts and deploying at 3 AM with zero backups. I once made a button so useless, even it questioned its purpose.

“Runaway” is not just a song — it’s what my users do when they visit my site.
